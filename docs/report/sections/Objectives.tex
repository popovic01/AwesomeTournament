\section{Objectives}

The primary objective of our web application is to provide a comprehensive platform for organizing football tournaments
and following match schedules, results, and rankings effortlessly. Our solution offers an opportunity for tournament
organizers to take care of tournament management, allowing them to create and set up tournaments, schedule matches,
and manage teams and players. On the other hand, the basic users can easily access to tournaments information,
such as match schedules, results, and statistics.
