\subsection{REST API Summary}

Here is the list of REST API implemented.

\begin{longtable}{|p{.425\columnwidth}|p{.1\columnwidth} |p{.35\columnwidth}|} 
\hline
\textbf{URI} & \textbf{Method} & \textbf{Description}  \\\hline
/auth/login & POST &  Sends email and password to be checked\\ \hline
api/users & POST &  Creates a new user\\ \hline
api/users/[user] & GET &  Returns [user] fields\\ \hline
api/users/[user] & PUT &  Updates [user] fields\\ \hline
api/users/[user] & DELETE &  Deletes [user]\\ \hline
api/tournaments & GET &  Returns a list of all tournaments\\ \hline
api/tournaments & POST &  Creates a new tournament connected to the user making the request\\ \hline
api/tournaments/[tournament] & GET &  Returns [tournament] fields\\ \hline
api/tournaments/[tournament] & PUT &  Updates [tournament] fields\\ \hline
api/tournaments/[tournament] & DELETE &  Deletes [tournament]\\ \hline
api/tournaments/[tournament]/teams & GET &  Returns a list of all teams in [tournament]\\ \hline
api/tournaments/[tournament]/teams & POST &  Create a team connected to [tournament]\\ \hline
api/tournaments/[tournament]/matches & GET &  Returns the list of all matches in [tournament]\\ \hline
api/tournaments/[tournament]/ranking & GET &  Returns the ranking of [tournament]\\ \hline
api/tournaments/[tournament]/scorers\_ranking & GET &  Returns the scorers ranking of [tournament]\\ \hline
api/matches/[match] & GET &  Returns [match] fields\\ \hline
api/matches/[match] & PUT &  Updates [match] fields\\ \hline
api/matches/[match]/events & GET &  Returns a list of all the events in [match]\\ \hline
api/matches/[match]/events & POST &  Creates a new event connected to [match]\\ \hline
api/events/[event] & PUT &  Updates an event\\ \hline
api/events/[event] & DELETE &  Deletes an event\\ \hline
api/teams/[team] & GET &  Returns [team] fields\\ \hline
api/teams/[team] & PUT &  Updates [team] fields\\ \hline
api/teams/[team] & DELETE &  Deletes [team]\\ \hline

api/teams/[team]/players & POST & Creates a new player in [team]\\ \hline
api/teams/[team]/players & GET &  Returns a list of all players in [team]\\ \hline
api/players/[player] & GET &  Returns the fields of [player]\\ \hline
api/players/[player] & PUT &  Updates [player] fields\\ \hline
api/players/[player] & DELETE &  Deletes [player]\\ \hline
\caption{REST API Summary}
\label{tab:termGlossary}
\end{longtable}
